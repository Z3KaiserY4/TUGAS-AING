Ilya Yefimovich Repin

5 August 1844 – 29 September 1930

Ilya Repin was a talented painter of the Peredvizhniki School, who was held up by the Soviet government as an artist to be imitated by the new school of Socialist Realists. He is regarded as one of the most important Russian painters.
